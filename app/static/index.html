Before you continue

This tool helps you research NZ building consent requirements under the Building Act 2004, Schedule 1 exempt building work, and district plan zoning rules.

This tool does not constitute professional or legal advice. No building certifier-client or solicitor-client relationship is created by using this tool. The developer accepts no liability for decisions made based on information provided.

Zone data is indicative only. District plan zone boundaries are sourced from council open data portals and may not reflect the latest plan changes. Always confirm your zone and applicable rules directly with your council before starting any project.

AI-generated answers may be inaccurate, incomplete, or outdated. Building Act exemptions and district plan rules are complex and fact-specific. Always confirm your specific situation with your local council, a licensed building certifier, or a chartered professional engineer before starting work.

For guidance on your project, contact MBIE Building Performance or your local council's duty planner.
